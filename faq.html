Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ORIGYN, our products, and how the QR experience works.

About ORIGYN

What is ORIGYN?

ORIGYN is a storytelling apparel brand that connects people to place through clothing that carries real stories. Each piece represents a specific town or region and includes a QR code that unlocks oral history, archival photos, and mini-documentaries you won't find anywhere else.

Why is there a Y in ORIGYN?

The Y represents two things: You and Why. This isn't just about where a place comes from—it's about your connection to it and why these stories matter. Every origin is personal.

Where do the stories come from?

We work with local historians, archivists, community members, and primary sources to research and verify every story. All claims are sourced and credited. We don't make things up or rely on generic Wikipedia entries—we dig deeper.

Products & QR Codes

How does the QR code work?

Every ORIGYN piece includes a QR code on the hangtag and inside label. Simply open your phone's camera app, point it at the code, and tap the notification that appears. No special app needed. You'll be taken to an immersive story page with photos, videos, audio, and historical timelines.

What fabrics do you use?

We use a 60% organic cotton / 40% hemp blend for most of our apparel. Hemp is naturally durable, breathable, and has a lower environmental impact than conventional cotton. The fabric gets softer with every wash and is built to last years, not seasons.

How do your products fit?

Our pieces are designed with a relaxed, classic fit. Check our Size Guide for detailed measurements. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a more comfortable fit.

How should I care for my ORIGYN apparel?

Machine wash cold with like colors. Tumble dry low or hang dry. The hemp-cotton blend gets softer over time. Avoid bleach and harsh detergents. Iron on low heat if needed.

Shipping & Returns

How long does shipping take?

Orders are processed within 1-3 business days. Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Expedited options are available at checkout. You'll receive a tracking number once your order ships.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we only ship within the United States. We're working on international shipping and will update this page when it's available.

What's your return policy?

We offer free returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and have all original tags attached. Visit our Shipping & Returns page for details.

Community & Contributions

Can I submit a story about my hometown?

Absolutely! We rely on community members to help us tell stories accurately. Visit our Community page to submit stories, photos, or corrections.

How do I request my town to be featured?

Use the Request Your Town form on our Community page. Tell us why your place matters and we'll prioritize based on community interest and story potential.

Do you credit contributors?

Yes. Every contributor—whether they shared a photo, told a story, or provided historical context—is credited in our Sources & Credits page and on the relevant story pages.

Sustainability

Is ORIGYN sustainable?

We prioritize hemp blends because they have lower environmental impact than conventional cotton in lifecycle comparisons. We work with certified supply partners who meet audited standards. We're not perfect, but we're honest about what we use and why. Read our full approach on our Sustainability page.

Where are your products made?

We manufacture with Hemp Fortex, a vertically integrated facility with certifications including GOTS, OCS, RCS, ISO 9001, and BSCI/Fair Trade. We prioritize audited standards and traceability throughout our supply chain.

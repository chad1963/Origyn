1818

Florence Is Founded

Established along the Tennessee River as a strategic river port. Named after Florence, Italy, with hopes of becoming the "Athens of the South."

1959

FAME Studios Opens

Rick Hall opens FAME Recording Studios in nearby Muscle Shoals. The "Muscle Shoals Sound" begins to take shape, attracting legends like Aretha Franklin, The Rolling Stones, and Wilson Pickett.

Accessibility

ORIGYN is committed to making our website accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities.

Our Commitment

We believe that everyone should be able to access our stories, products, and mission. We're actively working to ensure that origyn.com meets or exceeds the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards.

Accessibility is an ongoing effort, and we're continuously improving. If you encounter any barriers, please let us know.

Current Accessibility Features

  • Semantic HTML: We use proper heading hierarchy and HTML5 semantic elements for screen readers.
  • Keyboard Navigation: All interactive elements are accessible via keyboard.
  • Alt Text: All images include descriptive alt text.
  • Color Contrast: Text meets WCAG AA contrast ratio requirements.
  • Responsive Design: Our site adapts to different screen sizes and zoom levels.
  • Readable Fonts: Clean, legible typography with adjustable text sizes.
  • Skip Links: Navigation skip links for screen reader users.
  • ARIA Labels: Proper ARIA labels for interactive elements.

Known Issues & Ongoing Improvements

We're actively working on:

  • Adding transcripts for all audio and video content
  • Improving focus indicators for keyboard navigation
  • Enhancing form error messaging
  • Ensuring all PDF documents are screen-reader accessible
  • Regular accessibility audits and testing

Report an Accessibility Issue

If you experience any difficulty accessing our website or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us:

  • Email: accessibility@origyn.com
  • Phone: (Coming soon)
  • Contact Form: Contact Us

When reporting an issue, please include:

  • The page URL where you experienced the issue
  • A description of the problem
  • The browser and assistive technology you're using

We'll respond within 2 business days and work to resolve the issue as quickly as possible.

Third-Party Content

Some content on our site is provided by third parties (e.g., Shopify checkout, embedded videos). While we strive to ensure all integrated services are accessible, we may not have full control over their accessibility features. We're actively working with our partners to ensure the best possible experience for all users.

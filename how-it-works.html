FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need an app to scan the QR code?
No. Just open your phone's camera app and point it at the code. A link will appear that takes you directly to the story page in your browser.
What if the QR code gets damaged?
Every garment has QR codes in two places: the hang tag and inside the collar label. Even if one gets damaged, the other should work. And the story pages are always accessible directly through our website.
Will the story pages stay online?
Yes. We design our URLs to be permanent. Even if we redesign the website, the links will always work. That's important when you've printed physical QR codes.
Do you track when I scan?
We track aggregate scan counts to understand which stories resonate, but we don't collect personal data. We don't know who you are - just that someone scanned a Florence code.
Can I access stories without buying anything?
Yes. Our story pages are publicly accessible. You can browse them anytime at origyn.com/stories. The QR code is just a convenient way to get there from your shirt.
